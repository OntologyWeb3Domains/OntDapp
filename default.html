FAQ

What is blockchain domains?
A blockchain domain is an asset class on the blockchain. Blockchain domains are designed to resolve your web address and wallet using smart contracts.
Can I transfer my ont domain?
Yes. ont domain are ERC721 compliant NFTs, you can transfer it with wallet that supports ERC721.
Does ont domain compatible with traditional ICANN domains?
No, ont domain is built with blockchain technology.
